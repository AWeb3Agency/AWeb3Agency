Web3 Superheroes

Why using Web3 based solutions

Web2 has helped onboard billions of people to the Web and created a stable, robust infrastructure on which we all rely on. At the same time, a handful of centralized entities (a.k.a. Google, Facebook, Apple, ...) have a stronghold on large and important pieces of the Web, unilaterally deciding what should and should not be allowed.

Web3 is the answer to this dilemma. Instead of a Web monopolized by large technology companies, Web3 embraces decentralization and is being built, operated, and owned by its users. Web3 puts power in the hands of individuals rather than corporations.


  • 01
    Decentralization

    instead of having big tech owning and controlling the internet, ownership gets distributed amongst its builders and users.

  • 02
    Permissionless

    everyone has equal access to participate in Web3, and no one gets excluded.

  • 03
    New Economy

    it uses cryptocurrency for spending and sending money online instead of relying on the outdated infrastructure of banks and payment processors.
